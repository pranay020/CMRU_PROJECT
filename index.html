    • Welcome To ICVMWT-2021

    It gives us immense pleasure to invite you to join ICVMWT-2021 (International Conference On VLSI & Microwave and Wireless Technologies) to be held at one of the most ancient and holy cities of India “Gorakhpur”, Uttar Pradesh from March 20–21 March, 2021. The conference is being jointly organised by Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology Gorakhpur, J.K. Institute of Applied Physics and Technology, University of Allahabad and Manipal University Jaipur with an aim to provide a platform for researchers, scientists, technocrats, academicians and engineers to exchange their innovative ideas and new challenges being faced in the field of Electronics Engineering. This Conference will be a platform to focus on the core technological developments in the emerging fields like wireless communication, RF microwave/Radar, VLSI, Optical Communication and so on.

    Publishing & Indexing Information: All accepted & presented papers of the conference by duly registered authors will be submitted to Springer book Series “Lecture Notes in Electrical Engineering (LNEE)” (https://www.springer.com/series/7818) and indexed in EI Compendex and Scopus databases.
